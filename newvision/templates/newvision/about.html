Our history

  • Year of foundation: 1990

    The company was founded in 1990 with the aim of providing high-quality cleaning and maintenance services.

  • Year of successful operation: 1992

    In 1992, we began to successfully complete our first projects and gain the trust of our clients thanks to our professional team and excellent quality of service.

  • Expansion: 1995

    In 1995, we successfully expanded the geography of our services and began serving clients in other regions.

  • Introduction of new technologies: 2000

    In 2000, the company actively introduced modern technologies and cleaning products to improve efficiency and quality of service.

  • Ecological initiatives: 2003

    In 2003, we began to actively implement environmental initiatives in our work and use environmentally-friendly cleaning products.

  • Technical renovations: 2006

    In 2006, the company carried out a major upgrade of equipment and technical base to provide more high performance.

  • Award "Best Cleaning Company": 2011

    In 2011, we received the prestigious "Best Cleaning Company" award for outstanding achievements in the industry.

  • 25th anniversary: 2016

    In 2016, we celebrated our company's 25th anniversary with pride and gratitude to our customers and employees.

  • Leading the market: 2023

    At the moment, we continue to be a leading company in the cleaning industry and strive to reach new heights in customer service.
